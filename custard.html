Custard Doughnuts

Dough Ingredients

  • 2 cups and 3 tbsp of flour
  • 3tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp salt
  • 1/4 tbsp nutmeg
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/3 stick melted butter
  • 1 packet of dry yeast

Dough Direction

Mix all the dry ingredients first. Then in a diffrent bowl add all the wet ingredients and the yeast. Then add the wet to the dry slowly and mix with dough kneeder or kneed by hand. Transfer dough to bowl covered in oil. Let the dough rest for two hours.

Custard Ingredients

  • 2 2/3 cups of milk
  • 3 whisked eggs(make sure to whisk eggs before adding to milk
  • 3/4 cup and 2tbsp of sugar
  • 5tbs cornstarch
  • 2tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Custard Directions

Add sugar, salt and cornstarch to pot.Then whisk milk int sugar mix. Then add whisked eggs. The pot should be kept at a medium high heat. Keep whisking until the mixture thickens. Once the mixture has thickened turn off heat and add vanilla. Then put mixture into a bowl cover with plastic wrap make sure the plastic is touching the surface of the custard so a skin doesn't form. Then place into the fridge to cool

Doughnut directions

Roll out dough. Then use a circular cookie cutter to cut dough into pieces. Cover dough for another 30 min before frying until golden brown on both sides. Once the doughnuts are done coat in sugar. Then open the doughnut with a small hole and wiggle to create space in the doughnut for the custard. Then pipe the chilled custard into the doughnuts.
