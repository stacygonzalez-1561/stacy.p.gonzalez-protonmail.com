Nutella Brownie Cookies

Brownie Ingredients

  • 1 cup dark chocolate
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (room temp)
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup Nutella
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Brownie directions

Microwave butter and dark chocolate for 30 seconds. Mix until well combined.

Add egg, brown sugar, and salt. Mix until bubbly on top.

Add brownie mixture to egg and sugar mixture. Mix until well combined

Add vanilla and nutella to brownie mixture.

Sift flour, baking powder and baking soda into mixture. Combine until smooth.

Seal and refrigerate for at least an hour.

Cookie Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 cup cake flour

Whisk room temperature butter until custerd like consistency.

Add sugar and salt to butter. Mix until well combined.

Add egg and vanilla. Mix until well combined.

Sift cake flour into mixture. Mix until combined.

Wrap dough and refrigerate for an hour

Baking Directions

Cut cookie dough into tablespoon size and roll into balls.

Do the same to the brownie dough

Flaten the cookie dough in the palm of your hand

Add brownie dough to the center of the cookie dough

Wrap cookie dough around the brownie

Press cookie down into a disc shape

Bake cookies at 325 fahrenheit for 20 minutes
