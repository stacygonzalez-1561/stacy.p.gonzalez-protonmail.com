SUGAR COOKIES

Dough ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 3 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp almond
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 1/2 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cream of tartar

    • Dough directions

    Mix butter, sugar, and cream cheese with mixer until smooth (no sugar grains). Mix egg, vanilla and almond until combined.Fold salt, flour, baking poweder, and cream of tartar before mixing lightly (don't over work the dough) Then put the dough in plastic wrap and put in refrigerator for two hours.

    Rolling out the dough and baking

    Roll out the dough to 1/4 of an inch. Flour your working surface. Flour your cookie cutters before cutting the cookies. Put the cut cookies in the fridge for 20 before baking at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

    Royal icing ingredients

    • 4 cups of powdered sugar.
    • a little bit of water as needed
    • 1/2 tsp of salt
    • 1 tsp of vanilla
    • 1/4 cup of pasteurized egg whites

    Royal icing directions

    Incorporate powder sugar slowly to the egg whites. Add water as needed to thin icing. You must work quickly to prevent the icing from drying out.
